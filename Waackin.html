Waacking

Waacking (also whacking, posing, punking, voguing) is a form of street dance created in the LGBT clubs of Los Angeles[1][2] during the 1970s disco era.[3] The style is typically done to 70s disco music[4] and is mainly distinguishable by its rotational arm movements, posing and emphasis on expressiveness.
